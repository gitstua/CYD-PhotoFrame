Do You Need CH340 Drivers?

If your computer is unable to recognize the ESP32 when you try to flash the firmware, you may need to install the CH340 USB-to-serial drivers.

How to Install the CH340 Driver:

  • Download the CH340 driver for your operating system:
    • Windows Driver (CH340)
    • Mac OS Driver (CH340)
  • Unzip the downloaded file.
  • Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
  • Once installed, reconnect your ESP32 and try flashing the firmware again.

After installing the drivers, your computer should recognize the ESP32, and you can proceed with flashing the firmware.
